WAREHOUSE VOCUBLURY

Picker (Πικαδόρος) : The worker whose job is to collect goods based on the directions he gets from a portable machine.

Παλέτα : Pallet, is a flat transport structure that supports goods in a stable fashion while being lifted.

Αποθήκη : Warehouse

Κιβώτιο : Box

Ράφι : Rack

Θέση : Location

Διάδρομος : Aisle, any passageway within a storage area.

Barcode : A machine-readable code in the form of numbers and a pattern of parallel lines of varying widths, printed on a commodity and used especially in warehouses.

Θέση Picking : A warehouse location which is easy accessible by a pedestrian picker.

Ανατροφοδοσία : Picking location refill

Ράμπα : Ramp, the door of the warehouse where goods are loading or unloading from trucks. Alternative, ramp is the place where the goods that will be put in a specific truck are gather.

Dock : Ramp, the door of the warehouse where goods are loading or unloading from trucks.

Παλετοφόρο : Pallet trucker, usually electric moving vehicle which pickers use to transfer pallets or kola with goods inside the warehouse.

RF (Scanner) : The most common portable machine that pickers use to get directions which location to visit and what good, in which quantity to collect.

Κλαρκ : Clark, forklift, all types of vehicles that a warehouse use to lift goods.

Reach Truck : A special type of clark.

VNA : A special type of clark.

WMS : Warehouse Management System – systems used to effectively manage processes, activities within the warehouse.

SSCC : Serial Shipping Container Code, a combination of numbers that defines a global unique id number for any transfer type of package (Pallet, Box, Cube etc).

Κωδικός Είδους : Item Code, SKU, Stock Keeping Unit, this is a product or service identification number assigned to a unique item by a retailer.  The SKU can be a number used internally or may be linked to the UPC (Universal Product Code) or EAN (European Article Number).

Λίστα Συλλογής : Picking list, a list of stock items to be picked to fill an order.

Κέντρο διανομής : Distribution center.

Cross Docking : Merchandise that is received at the warehouse or DC is not put away but prepared for shipment to retail stores.
